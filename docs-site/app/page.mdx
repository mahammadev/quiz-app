# QuizCreator Documentation

Welcome to the QuizCreator documentation. QuizCreator is a **personal quiz creation platform** that turns any text or PDF into an interactive quiz for study, teaching, or exam prep.

## What is QuizCreator?

QuizCreator allows you to:

- **Upload PDFs** and automatically extract questions
- **Paste text** to generate quizzes with AI
- **Share quizzes** with anyone via a simple link
- **Track progress** with leaderboards and mistake logging
- **Manage teams** with organizations and role-based access

## What This is NOT

- ❌ Not a quiz marketplace — no "explore" or "trending" pages
- ❌ Not a social platform — no followers, comments, or public profiles
- ❌ Not an LMS — no courses, grading workflows, or student rosters

## Target Users

| Persona | Use Case |
|---------|----------|
| **Individual Learner** | Upload study materials, generate quizzes, track progress |
| **Teacher/Tutor** | Create quizzes from curriculum, share with students |
| **Exam Preparer** | Convert past papers to interactive tests |
